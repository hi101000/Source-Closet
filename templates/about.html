About me and this site

I'm a high school student in the USA who interested in both history and coding. This website is meant to make historical sources more accessible to the general public, who may not feel like pouring through bibliographies or looking through archives for what they want. This website likely will not help very much with geneaology, since those records are too numerous for me to effectively make available here; the focus of this website is more geared towards general historical research. All materials hosted on this website are, unless otherwise stated, free from copyright and in the public domain. Feel free to download and distribute them as you wish.
