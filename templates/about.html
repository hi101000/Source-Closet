About me and this site

I'm a high school student in the USA who interested in both history and coding. Therefore, I thought I'd contribute my two cents to the history nerd community by creating this website. Here, you will find historical sources from across history (though focusing more on the 20th and 21st century). I really just upload the sources as I come across them. As such, the exact list of sources and the topic of new sources will be kind of biased by whatever I'm researching at the moment. Nonetheless, I hope to make a meaningful contribution to the lives of people around the world who have an interest in history and save them the hours of hair-pulling which I had to endure to find some of these sources.
